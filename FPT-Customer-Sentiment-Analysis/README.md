# FPT Play — Customer Sentiment Analysis

## Overview

Customer sentiment analysis project using Machine Learning and NLP to evaluate user experience and identify service improvement opportunities from customer comments.

## Business Problem

- Measure customer satisfaction
- Identify negative feedback patterns
- Support data-driven service improvement

## Tech Stack

- Python
- MongoDB
- Pandas
- PhoBERT (VinAI)
- Natural Language Processing

## Key Findings

- Positive comments outnumber negative comments by approximately 3:1.
- Sports content generated the highest interaction volume.
- Android users showed a relatively higher negative feedback rate.
- Ho Chi Minh City contained the largest concentration of customer feedback.

## Business Value

- Identify customer pain points from unstructured data.
- Support product and service quality improvements.
- Provide data-driven recommendations for customer experience management.
